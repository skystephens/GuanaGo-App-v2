Biography

Born from the Sound of the Island

Kalea started her musical journey in the dense mangroves of the island's north shore. Influenced by traditional percussionists and the rising wave of electronic music, she forged a unique sound that bridges the gap between the organic and the digital.

Her sets are not just performances; they are rituals. Designed to reconnect the audience with nature through frequency and rhythm.
